Stop Scammers.

ScamWatch is an open awareness project helping victims and scambaiters identify and expose scams.

What is ScamWatch?

ScamWatch is a research and awareness platform documenting scams, fake websites, and common tools used by fraud operations.

Phishing campaigns and scam websites now number in the thousands and often impersonate major brands like Amazon, Google, or Microsoft to steal data and money.

Recently Exposed Scams

Tech Support Scam Call Centers

Fraud operations impersonate companies like Microsoft and convince victims to install remote access software such as AnyDesk or TeamViewer.

Fake Ecommerce Stores

Thousands of fake online stores appear during events like Black Friday, using copied product images and huge discounts to steal payment information.

SMS Phishing (Smishing)

Mass campaigns send text messages pretending to be banks or shipping companies with malicious login pages.

Common Tools Used by Scammers

AnyDesk

Remote desktop software abused by scammers to gain control of victims computers.

TeamViewer

Often used in tech support scams to remotely access devices and steal financial data.

Phishing Kits

Prebuilt packages that create fake login pages and capture credentials.

VoIP Call Systems

Scammers use VoIP and call center software to impersonate banks and tech companies.
